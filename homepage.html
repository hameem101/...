about us

we are a single location retail outlet for locally produced food and drink, we offer collective marketing for local farmers and food producers.

our local retail shop is known for fresh, high quality food and drinks. we love serving our local community with seasonal fruits, vegtables and important essentials. we also work close with farmers and suppliers to help us keep our shelves fresh and sustainable. we serve many ingridients if you also love home cooked meals. unlike big supply chains we welcome everyone in store and make them feel like they are a part of something by helping out a local business that contributes to the local area. we aim to make every visit expedential and a good customer experience.
